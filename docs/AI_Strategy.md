# SmartGuard AI Strategy

## Vision

SmartGuard will evolve from a sensor-based monitoring platform into an AI-assisted theft detection and prevention system.

---

## Phase 1 - Rule Based Intelligence

The first prototype will use predefined rules.

Examples:

Door Opened
=
LOW Risk

Door Opened + Motion
=
HIGH Risk

Door Opened + Motion + After Hours
=
CRITICAL Risk

---

## Phase 2 - Camera Assisted Detection

Camera captures images when events occur.

Process:

Event Trigger
↓
Camera Snapshot
↓
Image Upload
↓
AI Analysis
↓
Risk Score

Questions AI will answer:

- Is a person visible?
- How many people are visible?
- Is movement detected?
- Is the event suspicious?

---

## Phase 3 - Behavioral Analysis

SmartGuard begins learning patterns.

Examples:

- Normal opening times
- Normal maintenance schedules
- Normal personnel access

Anomalies increase risk scores.

Example:

Cabinet usually opened:
08:00 - 17:00

Cabinet opened:
02:15

Risk increases automatically.
